Ripping HR KPICS

Requirements

1. Firefox - https://www.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/new/
2. Tapermonkey - https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/tampermonkey/
	*Greasemonkey works too
3. DownThemAll! - https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/downthemall/
4. DownThemAll! Anti-Container - https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/downthemall-anticontainer/
5. DownThemAll! Anti-Container plugins - https://mega.nz/#!B2RilCpB!0nHqfbp8vZYyGC7EeFEChCnPJYD1cQVy1KzTvpoYmKI

Instructions

1. Open DownThemAll! Preferences 
2. Click the 'Anti-Container' page then 'Install Plugin' at the bottom
3. Navigate to where you extracted DownThemAll! Anti-Containers.zip
4. Install all 3 .json files

Usage -- see https://imgur.com/a/jmBu7

1. Navigate to a webpage you want to rip images from (http://XXXXX.tistory.com/XXX for example)
2. Open DownThemAll! and click the 'Links' tab
3. In the 'Fast Filtering' field, type "original" 
4. Select your download directory and press Start!
